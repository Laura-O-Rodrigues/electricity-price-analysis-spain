# Electricity Market Price Analysis (Spain & Portugal, 2023–2025)

## Objective

This project analyzes the evolution of monthly average electricity prices 
in the Iberian market (Spain and Portugal) between 2023 and 2025.

The goal is to identify price trends, volatility patterns, and potential 
implications for energy procurement strategies and market risk assessment.

---

## Data Source

- OMIE (Operador del Mercado Ibérico de Energía)
- Annual market files (monthly aggregated data)
- Processed using Python (pandas)

---

## Methodology

1. Raw annual Excel files (2023–2025) were cleaned and standardized.
2. Monthly average prices for Spain and Portugal were extracted.
3. Datasets were consolidated into a structured panel dataset.
4. Time series analysis was prepared for trend and volatility assessment.

---

## Key Analytical Focus

- Year-over-year price comparison
- Monthly volatility patterns
- Spain vs Portugal price convergence
- Market stabilization post-energy crisis

---

## Tools Used

- Python (pandas)
- Data cleaning and transformation
- Structured dataset generation for further modeling

---

## Strategic Relevance

Understanding electricity price evolution is critical for:

- PPA structuring
- Industrial energy procurement strategy
- Risk exposure assessment
- Policy and regulatory impact analysis

This dataset can support further modeling, forecasting, 
or scenario-based energy market simulations.
